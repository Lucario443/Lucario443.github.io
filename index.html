Feature list;
  1. God Mode - This disables damage, and also your guns are unusable too.
  2. ESP - A non-laggy esp that displays; HP, Gun Name, Username, Team (Can toggle additional info in settings)
  3. Aimbot - Aims at the closest enemy at a part that you pick. (UPDATED for the anti-cheat that kicks aimbotters)
  4. Aim Assist - There are two methods, FOV (Closest to cursor) and Hover/Aim locking (If your cursor falls under an enemy,m it will lock on to them)
  5. Teleport to Enemies - Since the latest update has broke regular Bring scripts, I made one that Teleports you behind the enemy and on to the next (I added a whitelist to only target one (or more) person(s) at a time)
  6. Auto Play - This is a fully automated bot that will; Walk to the enemy, aim at the enemy, shoot the enemy, repeat
  7. Auto Fix - A small module used by autoplay to switch to secondary (can be configured to primary)
  8. Rapid Fire - This module abuses a glitch to increase your firerate. Automatic shotguns anyone?
  9. Auto Walk - Walk to nearest enemy (Path finding/auto jumping)
  10. Paths - Show a dotted line to the nearest enemy. (It displays the quickest route)
  11. Part Creation - Ever need an extra boost/part to jump on? No problem.
  12. Panic - One push and all modules instantly cease. It turns everything off.
  13. Derp - This will make you seem like a derpy noob that everyone lol's at.
  14. Auto Defuse - One push will automatically defuse the bomb. If you hold it, it toggles it and only defuses while game is in motion. (Works even when C4 is unplanted) [Before you ask, I did not get this from anywhere but Remote Spy] If it is hostage gamemode, you'll be given a hostage (Even on T team)
  15. 360 - If you ever landed a noscope, you may want to celebrate by spinning around. Or if you want to make derp even derpyer.
  16. Bhop - Automatically bhops. (Yes I'm aware this does not increase speed, that's why I made it slightly boost your speed)
  17. Teleport to Bombsite - Push it and you will teleport to A, push again, teleports to b, and re-cycle
  18. Help Menu - Essentially this list ingame, and settings.
  19. Respawner - Died too soon and wanted a second chance? Well this can give you infinite chances.
  20. Reset - Take 100 fall damage and die.
  21. Join CT - It will place you on the CT team
  22. Join T - It will place you on the T team
  23. Auto Join - This will place you on the team that has most wins, if it's a tie, it will be settled via settings
  24. Save Waypoints - Press to save a position for later teleportation.
  25. Load Waypoint - Press to restore a previously saved location.
  26. Clear Waypoints - Remove waypoints.
  27. Infinite Buy Time - This removes the time limit on buying. This also allows you to open buy menu from any where.
  28. Infinite Cash - Gun fountain anyone?
  29. Auto Respawn - If you take more than 25 damage, you'll be respawned and placed back into battle. (God mode with guns)
  30. Speed Changer - Change your speed from two options that can be changed in GUI
  31. Infinite Ammo - This will give your gun 3x the clips amount. If your ammo falls below its clip amount, you'll be given more ammo.
  32. Credit Grinder - This fully automated bot can get you up to 300 credits over night. It defuses and plants bombs.
  33. Flash Bang Spam - This will buy and throw flash bangs rapidly. Works well with infinite buy and infinite cash.
  34. Auto kill - This fully automated bot will kill every enemy within 30 seconds or less. (Now compatible with whitelist)
  35. No Clip - Removes clips from your body allowing movement through walls.
  36. Server Lag Switch - One push and people will start complaining of lag. Its handy for clearing servers. (Just tested, server shutdown after 30 seconds of use)
  37. Objective Walker - This very smart bot will walk you to your objective. if you T and you have bomb, you goto nearest bomb site. If you don't have bomb, it picks it up, or backs up whoever is holding it. If your CT and bomb is planted, it will walk you to the bombsite. If bomb is on ground, you'll walk to bomb. If bomb is in motion, you'll camp at which ever bombsite is closest TO the C4's carrier. In hostage maps, CT: it will walk you to nearest hostage. T: Walk to the nearest hostage closest to a CT player. If a user is carrying a hostage or you're carrying an hostage, you'll be walking to the rescue area.
  38. Plant C4 - Plants a C4 and depending on your settings, will plant a defusable c4 or a fail-proof c4 that CANNOT be defused without auto defuse. (Added a dynamic option which changes the way its planted based on your team)
  39. Skin Changer - If you ever need to look cool, my GUI will list all available skins and guns for you to choose from.
  40. Gun Modding - Infinite ammo, fully automatic 0 reload/switching time. Full auto AWP that shoots 8 bullets at a time anyone?
  41. X-Ray - A setting to allow targeting through walls.
